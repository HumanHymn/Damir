About Me & Skills

I'm Damir- a Croat living in Mexico, speak three languages, technically trained as a civil engineer, though I pivoted to the internet about 15 years ago, and never really looked back.

Since then it's mostly been digital marketing: building websites, content strategies, creating branding from scratch, SEO, social media, blogs, video, influencer outreach, market research, data analysis, product fulfilment, grant finding and applications... More recently I've been leaning into audits, consultancy, deep market and customer insights.

I'm also neck-deep into AI, since the time it was still called machine learning. I'm a beta tester, early adopter, a pusher. Vibe coding is my latest obsession, tho I also dabbled in coding before AI made it somewhat easy.

The thread that stiches it all together is my curiosity, a love of collaboration, and an obsession with creating systems, organized workflows and meeting deadlines.
